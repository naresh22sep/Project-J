Role Guidelines
Role Levels
  • 1-30: Basic to advanced users
  • 31-60: Moderators and managers
  • 61-90: Administrators
  • 91-100: System-level access
Naming Convention
  • Name: Use lowercase with underscores
  • Display Name: Use proper capitalization
  • Be descriptive: content_manager, not cm
Permission Assignment
  • Follow principle of least privilege
  • Group related permissions together
  • System permissions are critical - assign carefully
  • Test role functionality after creation
Tip: Start with minimal permissions and add more as needed. It's easier to grant permissions than to revoke them.
